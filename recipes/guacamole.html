GUACAMOLE!

here's what you need:

  • 3 avacados, mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1/2 cup diced onions
  • 3 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

here's what you do:

  1. mash avacados, lime juice, and salt together in a bowl
  2. mix in tomatoes, onion, cilantro, and garlic
  3. stir in cayenne pepper
  4. eat the whole bowl
