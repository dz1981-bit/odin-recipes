MEXICAN HOT CHOCOLATE!!

here's what you need:

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 tablespoon Mexican hot chocolate tablet
  • 1 dried red chile de arbol, lightly toasted

here's what you do:

step 1

  1. place milk and chocolate in a small saucepan over medium -low heat
  2. simmer and stir until chocolate is melted but not boling (about 5 minutes)

step 2

  1. pour chocolate mixture into a blender
  2. cover blender lid with a potholder and hold it down
  3. blend until foamy

step 3

  1. break chile into 2 or 3 pieces
  2. discard the seeds
  3. drop chile pieces into the hot chocolate mixture

step 4

  1. drink immeditately
  2. feel the burn
  3. feel life
