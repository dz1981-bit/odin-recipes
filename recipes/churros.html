CHURROS!

here's what you need:

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 quarts oil for frying
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

here's what you do:

step 1

  1. combine water, 2 1/2 tablespoons sugar, salt, and 2 tablesoons vegetable oil in a saucepan over medium heat
  2. bring to a boil and remove from heat
  3. stir in flour until mixture forms a ball

step 2

  1. heat oil in a deep fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)

step 3

  1. transfer dough from step 1 to a pastry bag fitted with a medium star tip
  2. pipe 5- to 6-inch strips of dough into the hot oil
  3. cook until golden brown
  4. use slotted spoon to transfer churros to paper towel to drain

step 4

  1. combine 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon
  2. roll drained churros in cinnamon and sugar mixture

step 5

  1. eat a whole bunch of hot, steamy churros
  2. smile in delight and enjoy life
